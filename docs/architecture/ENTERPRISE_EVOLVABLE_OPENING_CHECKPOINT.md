foundations that let SagaEngine evolve toward controlled studio workflows.

Allowed opening claims:

- Hedef 3 governance, threat, and policy evidence work may begin.
- Enterprise-evolvable means foundation work, not an enterprise-ready platform.
- Policy evaluation in Block B is local/report-only unless a later phase proves
