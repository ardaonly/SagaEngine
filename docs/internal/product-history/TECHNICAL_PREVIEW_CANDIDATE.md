# Historical Technical Preview Candidate Status Note

This note preserves the former product candidate-status material. It is
historical context, not current product truth and not current onboarding
material.

The useful idea is that limited Linux distribution evidence can aggregate staged
layout, archive, checksum, preflight, smoke, and candidate reports. Those
artifacts are review evidence only. They do not prove production readiness,
enterprise readiness, a verified final release, full distribution
certification, full editor workflow, full Visual Blocks UI, full gameplay or
runtime validation, full collaboration, or cloud workspace support.
