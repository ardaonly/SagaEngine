Licensing

LICENSE is the repository’s authoritative primary license text. Saga-owned engine and editor source is currently licensed under MPL-2.0. SPDX license texts and the authoritative third-party notice are under LICENSES/. The former Editor-restricted license is historical and is not part of current policy or the canonical license bundle. LICENSE_POLICY.toml, LICENSE_TEXT_SOURCES.toml, .reuse/dep5, and generated checksum data define machine-checked coverage.

Generated output inherits licensing only through explicit policy. Vendor and imported code retain their upstream notices. Product direction does not authorize proprietary, leaked, tainted, or license-unclear source.
