Not implemented and non-claims

Current evidence does not establish a finished editor journey, complete standalone gameplay loop, production multiplayer or persistence, production security, public SDK, stable binary ABI, clean-machine distribution, installer or updater, marketplace, hosted collaboration, managed operations, or commercial support commitment.

Rendering code and focused GPU tests do not establish backend parity, device-loss recovery, driver certification, performance certification, or production renderer readiness. Stress infrastructure does not establish scale or soak certification. Samples and fixtures are not finished games.

C# and Visual Blocks foundations do not establish arbitrary untrusted-code execution, a secure user-script sandbox, lossless round-trip authoring, or a finished debugging and deployment experience.

Architecture intent describes desired boundaries, not implemented product capability. Positive claims require discoverable current evidence. File presence proves presence only; historical reports, plans, and names are not current capability evidence.
