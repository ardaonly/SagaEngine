# Not implemented and non-claims

Useful subsystem foundations may exist while the corresponding end-to-end capability remains unproven. Removing an item from this page requires current, discoverable evidence for the complete claim.

## Runtime, content, and samples

Current evidence does not establish a complete project-create, edit, run, save, build, and package journey; a finished standalone gameplay loop; a production asset import/cook/cache/migration pipeline; clean-machine packaged-game operation; or a finished sample or tutorial game. Fixtures, staged packages, manifests, and bounded playable evidence retain those narrower classifications.

## Online and persistent state

Current evidence does not establish a complete multiplayer gameplay loop; production replication, reconciliation, reconnect, restart recovery, or persistence durability; community-server administration; hostile-network security; anti-cheat or abuse prevention; or scale, soak, concurrency, and availability certification.

## Editor, scripting, and trust

Current evidence does not establish a finished editor journey, complete scene/asset/prefab authoring, finished Visual Blocks UX, arbitrary or lossless C# round-tripping, or a finished debugging and deployment experience. It also does not establish arbitrary untrusted-code execution, a secure user-script sandbox, package signing and trust-chain enforcement, safe public user-code hosting, or complete provenance enforcement.

## Rendering, platforms, and release

Rendering code and focused GPU tests do not establish backend or vendor parity, complete device-loss and swapchain recovery, driver certification, frame-pacing or performance certification, a production renderer, or complete desktop/mobile/web/console/XR platform support. Current evidence also does not establish a public SDK, stable binary ABI, production distribution, installer, updater, marketplace, hosted collaboration, managed operations, or commercial support commitment.

## Evidence boundary

Stress infrastructure does not establish scale or soak certification, and focused tests do not establish permanent raw full-suite health. Reports are observations, not automatic enforcement or readiness guarantees.

Architecture intent describes desired boundaries, not implemented product capability. Positive claims require discoverable current evidence. File presence proves presence only; historical reports, plans, and names are not current capability evidence.
